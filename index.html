How to use:

  1. Go to your Gemini infographic.
  2. Click “Code” → “Share” → “Copy Contents”.
  3. Return here and paste (Ctrl+V or right-click).
  4. Click Generate PNG (may take a second).
  5. Check your downloads folder for the image.
